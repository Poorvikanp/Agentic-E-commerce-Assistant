SHIPPING POLICY

Standard Shipping:
- Free on orders above ₹999.
- ₹79 flat fee for orders below ₹999.
- Delivery in 4-6 business days.

Express Shipping:
- ₹199 flat fee.
- Delivery in 1-2 business days.
- Available for metro cities: Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi, Chennai, Hyderabad, Pune.

Same-Day Delivery:
- Available only in Bangalore.
- Order before 12 PM.
- ₹299 flat fee.
- Not available for items over 5kg.

Shipping carriers:
- We use BlueDart, Delhivery, and Ekart depending on your location.
- Tracking ID is shared via email and SMS once the order is shipped.

International Shipping:
- Currently not available.

Order processing time:
- Orders placed before 2 PM are processed the same day.
- Orders placed after 2 PM are processed the next business day.
- Processing does not happen on Sundays and public holidays.

Bulk orders (above 10 units):

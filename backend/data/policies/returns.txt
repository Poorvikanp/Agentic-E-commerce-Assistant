RETURN POLICY

We offer a 30-day return policy on all products.

Eligibility:
- Products must be unused, unwashed, and in original packaging.
- Tags must be intact.
- Sale items (marked with "SALE") are not eligible for return.
- Customized or personalized products cannot be returned.

How to initiate a return:
1. Go to your Orders page and click "Return Item".
2. Select a reason for return.
3. We will send a prepaid return label within 24 hours.
4. Drop the item at any BlueDart/Delhivery pickup point.
5. Refund is processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the item.

Refund methods:
- Original payment method: 5-7 business days.
- Store credit: within 24 hours (bonus 5% added).

Damaged or wrong items:
If you received a damaged or wrong product, contact support within 48 hours with photos. 
We will send a replacement or issue a full refund immediately, no need to return the item.

Exceptions:
- Hygiene products (yoga mats, shakers) can only be returned if sealed and unused.
- Electronics and smart devices have a 15-day return window.
